# NRI Mart Landing Page - Interactive Presentation

## Slide 1: Project Overview
**Title:** NRI Mart Landing Page - Your Bridge to Home

**Content:**
A comprehensive, professionally designed landing page for NRI Mart, a service platform connecting Non-Resident Indians with home delivery, shopping, and luggage management solutions. The website showcases a warm, culturally-aware design that emphasizes trust, personalization, and multilingual support.

**Key Metrics:**
- 8 Major Sections
- 4 Custom-Generated Visual Assets
- Responsive Design (Mobile, Tablet, Desktop)
- 4 Supported Languages
- 5 Core Benefits Highlighted

---

## Slide 2: Design Philosophy
**Title:** Modern Minimalist with Warm Accents

**Design Approach:**
The landing page employs a Modern Minimalist design philosophy with warm, culturally-connected colors that reflect Indian heritage and create emotional connection with the target audience.

**Color Palette:**
- Primary: Terracotta (#D97706) - Warmth and Heritage
- Secondary: Saffron (#F59E0B) - Prosperity and Joy
- Accent: Teal (#0F766E) - Trust and Stability
- Background: Warm Cream (#FFFBF0) - Welcoming Atmosphere

**Typography:**
- Headings: Playfair Display (Elegant, Distinctive)
- Body: Poppins (Friendly, Readable)

---

## Slide 3: Key Features Implemented
**Title:** Comprehensive Feature Set

**Core Sections:**
1. **Hero Section** - Warm background with clear value proposition and dual CTAs
2. **How It Works** - 4-step process: App Download → Product Request → Luggage Management → E-commerce Shopping
3. **Why Choose Us** - 4 key benefits with custom icons
4. **Multilingual Support** - Showcase of 4 languages (English, Hindi, Telugu, Tamil)
5. **Testimonials** - Customer reviews from NRIs worldwide
6. **Case Manager Information** - Emphasis on personalized service
7. **Call-to-Action Section** - Prominent download and contact buttons
8. **Footer** - Navigation and legal information
